ABOUT US

THE FANBOOK

2set4mil fanbook was a project in which all twosetters can participate. Consisting of stories, love letters, fanart, and fanfiction, the fanbook inclusively accommodated how fans express their love toward Brett and Eddy for their four million subscribers and nine years of YouTube career milestones. The title, Our Thoughts of You, was chosen right after they dropped Eddy’s composition, The Thought of Us, as our homage to their piece and to encompass our thoughts on how Brett and Eddy have inspired us in many ways. Like any classical piece, we designed the fanbook in four movements, each conveying different emotions so it flowed seamlessly into a big story.

THE PROCESS

The whole fanbook process was occupied in two months, starting from the registration on September 2 and printing out on November 2, 2022. This project was coordinated both in discord and twitter group so the participants could be updated with submission deadline’s dates and other announcements. Before inserting the submissions into the fanbook, the editorial team checked thoroughly all the submissions so they adequately were qualified according to the meticulous rules which were released during the registration period. On top of that, to inclusively engage participants, the editorial team conducted voting for full-page fanarts display and screening to receive feedback before printing out. In addition, to increase the chance this fanbook got noticed by Brett and Eddy, we put in lingling40hrs reddit. Finally, we brought the fanbook to TwoSet Violin & SSO concert so Brett and Eddy could receive it firsthand.
